# Privacy Policy for WebHook Wrangler

Last Updated: [Current Date]

## Overview

WebHook Wrangler is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection and Use

### What We DO Collect and Store:
- **Webhook Configurations**: Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- **Request History**: Stored locally in your browser
- **Current Page URL and Title**: Only when you explicitly click to send them
- **API Keys**: If you choose to use them, stored locally and encrypted

### What We DO NOT Collect:
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Usage statistics
- Analytics data
- Cookies
- IP addresses
- Location data

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

## Data Transmission

The only data transmission occurs when:
1. You explicitly click to send a URL to your configured webhook
2. The data is sent directly to your specified webhook URL
3. No data passes through our servers

## Permissions

Our extension requires minimal permissions:
- `activeTab`: To access the current tab's URL and title (only when you click to send)
- `storage`: To save your webhook configurations locally

## Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party services. All webhook connections are made directly to the endpoints you configure.

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally and persists until:
- You manually delete it through the extension interface
- You remove the extension
- You clear your browser data

## Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension's update notes.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have full control over all locally stored data through the extension's interface.

## Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Compliance

This extension complies with:
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
